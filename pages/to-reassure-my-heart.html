To Reassure My Heart

"This book inspires individuals with Quranic teachings."

The book "Messages from the Qur'an" by Adham Sharqawi is one of the books that combine religious thought and literary presentation in a smooth and simple style. In it, Sharqawi presents deep reflections on the Qur'anic verses in a style that is close to the reader, combining wisdom and simplicity, and presents ideas that touch on daily life issues in a spiritual style.
Book Content:
The book consists of a collection of messages inspired by the verses of the Holy Qur'an. Each message focuses on a specific topic such as: Faith and Patience: The writer addresses how to deal with trials in life and draws lessons from the stories of the prophets. Human Relations: Discusses how to build healthy relationships with others based on Islamic values. Justice and Mercy: Explains the concept of justice and its importance in life, and calls for compassion among people. Optimism and Trust in God: Focuses on the importance of faith in God's power and His wise management of matters. The writer's style: Adham Sharqawi speaks in an emotional and direct language that makes the reader feel close to the text. He uses stories and examples from reality and history to illustrate the messages he extracts from the Qur'an. Calls for understanding the Qur’an in a contemporary spirit, while adhering to religious principles.
Book Features:
Simple and effective style: Suitable for different age and cultural groups.
Short messages: Each message is self-contained, making the book ideal for quick reading and contemplation. Linking verses to daily life: Helps the reader see the Qur’an as a practical guide for his life. Who is the book suitable for? For those looking for a light and deep reading at the same time. For those who want to understand some Qur’anic meanings in a simple and inspiring way. For those who love Adham Sharqawi’s literary and emotional style.
